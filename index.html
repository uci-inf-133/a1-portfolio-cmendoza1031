About Me

I'm Cristian, a software engineer at UC Irvine with a passion for building technology that makes a real difference in people's lives. My goal in life is simple: build something that helps millions of people, and meet cool people along the way.

Currently, I'm pouring my energy into Clera, an AI-powered investment advisor that makes investing simple and helps you grow your money without needing to be an expert. Built with a multi-agent LangGraph system, a Next.js 15 frontend, FastAPI backend, and deployed across AWS ECS and Vercel with Supabase for auth and data.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me training for endurance events — I'm preparing for Oceanside 70.3 (March 2026) and Hyrox Las Vegas (February 2026) — or spending quality time with my family and friends.

Clera — AI Investment Advisor

My flagship project: a personal AI investment advisor that democratizes access to sophisticated investment advice. Built with a multi-agent LangGraph system powered by Claude, Clera can analyze markets, manage portfolios, execute trades, and provide personalized insights — making professional financial guidance accessible to everyone.

Key Features: Multi-agent AI orchestration, real-time portfolio tracking via WebSocket, brokerage integration (SnapTrade + Alpaca), trade execution with confirmation flows, and comprehensive market research capabilities.
